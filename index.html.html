Why Now

The cost of waiting is real — and avoidable.

Three specific things are working against PSG right now. All three are fixable. The question is how long they run before someone stops them.

Campaign spend without product visibility is leaving money on the table

PSG spends to promote job requisitions across LinkedIn and other channels. Without a behavioral layer, there's no way to know which campaigns drive candidates all the way through to hire — and which just generate leads that Compass loses somewhere in the funnel. Amplitude connects those dots and shows you exactly where to optimize spend.

Silent disengagement is easier to prevent than reverse

Customers who start using Compass less don't always say why. By the time it's obvious, recovery is hard. Amplitude surfaces behavioral warning signals weeks before they become churn — giving PSG's team time to act, not react. That's the difference between retaining a customer and replacing one.

Instrumentation is cleanest before you scale further

PSG is adding enterprise customers to Compass now. Instrumenting a product at this stage is a contained, manageable project. Every new client, integration, and architecture decision that happens before this gets done makes it a bigger lift — and delays the insight that would have improved every placement in the meantime.
